HST Filing

GST/HST filing is a mandatory requirement for registered Canadian businesses. The Canadian government guidelines stipulate that businesses with an annual income exceeding $30,000 must register for Goods and Services Tax (GST) and Harmonized Sales Tax (HST). Failure to collect or remit sales tax or file GST/HST returns by the deadline can result in substantial penalties imposed by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). At Accounting And Financials, we specialize in assisting businesses with GST/HST compliance. Our services include registering your business for GST/HST, providing guidance on the applicable sales tax rates, and advising you on the payment due dates.
